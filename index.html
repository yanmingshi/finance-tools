About FinCalcPro

FinCalcPro is a collection of 10 free financial calculators covering investment returns, compound interest, retirement planning, tax estimation, mortgage payments, and cost-of-living comparisons. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no server-side processing, no data collection, no signup.

What These Calculators Cover

  • Investment & Growth: ROI (with annualized CAGR), compound interest with configurable compounding frequency (daily to annual), and FIRE timeline based on your savings rate and withdrawal strategy.
  • Tax Estimation: Capital gains tax using 2025 federal brackets + state rates for all 50 states; crypto tax with short-term/long-term classification; salary-to-hourly conversion with take-home estimates per state.
  • Loans & Housing: Commercial mortgage with DSCR calculation; rent vs buy total-cost comparison including appreciation, tax deductions, and opportunity cost; debt payoff with avalanche vs snowball methods.
  • Cost Comparison: Side-by-side cost-of-living comparison between any two U.S. cities using BLS CPI data, including salary equivalent and housing cost breakdown.

Data Sources & Accuracy

Tax calculators reference 2025 IRS federal income tax brackets, standard deductions, and long-term capital gains rates. State tax rates are updated annually from state revenue department publications. Cost-of-living data is sourced from the Bureau of Labor Statistics Consumer Price Index. Mortgage calculations follow standard amortization formulas. All results are estimates — consult a tax professional or financial advisor before making decisions.

Privacy

Zero data leaves your browser. No cookies track your calculations. No analytics script watches what you type. The site is fully static — your inputs are processed by JavaScript on your device and never sent to any server.
